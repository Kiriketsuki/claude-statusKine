Powerline Style

Colored backgrounds on each segment, connected by arrow-shaped separators ( U+E0B0). Each segment's background color bleeds into the next separator. Dense, no empty space.

The Question

Powerline is a fundamentally different rendering approach — it replaces bridges with packed colored segments. sirmalloc's ccstatusline uses this style exclusively. Adding it to Chrysaki would mean maintaining two complete renderers behind an env var toggle.

It's a significant chunk of work. The question is whether to include it in this V2 release, stub it for later, or skip it entirely and keep Chrysaki as the sole style.
